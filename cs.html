Computer science The School of Computer Science has an outstanding team of faculty and staff, including renowned professors and researchers from around the world. The School's programs cover various areas of computer science, such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, software engineering, computer systems, and more. The School also offers undergraduate, graduate, and PhD programs, as well as professional certification programs such as Cisco, Microsoft, and Oracle, to name a few. The School of Computer Science has state-of-the-art computer labs and facilities that provide students with a variety of learning and research resources. The School also actively promotes student-industry collaboration in various hands-on projects and business partnerships such as software development, data analytics and artificial intelligence, among others, providing students with valuable hands-on and career development opportunities. In addition to education and research, the School of Computer Science also actively participates in various community activities and charity projects, such as organizing various open computer science courses, holding hackathon and programming competitions, and providing technical support and consulting services to the community, etc., making positive contributions to the society.
