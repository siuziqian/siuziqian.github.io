South Business School has a modern architectural design and a full range of interior facilities, including classrooms, offices, meeting rooms, a research center, a business library, and more. The School has an excellent faculty, including academics and business people from around the world who have extensive practical experience and research in their fields. South Business School offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate and MBA programs covering various business areas such as accounting, finance, marketing, human resources, business strategy and more. The School also offers various business training and professional certification programs such as ACCA, CIMA, CFA, etc. in order to help students obtain additional professional qualifications. South Business School also provides students with a wealth of business practice opportunities such as business internships, laboratory research, business consulting, business competitions, etc. to help students apply their theoretical knowledge into practice. In addition, the School has partnered with business leaders and organizations around the world to provide students with international business exchange opportunities such as exchange programs, short courses, and visiting scholar programs, among others. Overall, South Business School is a key business school at the University of Nottingham's Jubilee campus, offering a wealth of business education resources and opportunities that provide an ideal environment for students to study and practice business.
