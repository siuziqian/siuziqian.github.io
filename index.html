Rules Introduction Players start the game from the starting point and must follow a GPS-guided route to different areas. Upon arrival at each location, the geotourist app will play knowledge related to the building to learn about the history, culture and story of the school. Players need to follow clues in geotourist to find realistic objects placed in advance. artcode technology is used to scan realistic objects. After a successful scan, the player is given clues to the next location, which the player needs to visit and continue to collect treasures. Among the clues obtained, there are some wrong clues. Once the player succeeds in obtaining all the treasures and reaches the final destination within the specified time, the challenge is completed and the player is judged to be victorious.
