The Djanogly LRC is a modern building centrally located on the Jubilee campus and offers a wide range of books, journals, literature and multimedia resources covering a wide range of subject areas to meet the academic needs of students and faculty members. In addition, the Djanogly LRC offers a variety of online databases and electronic resources such as scholarly journals, specialized databases and e-books to provide students and faculty with easy access to information anytime, anywhere. Djanogly LRC also provides a variety of learning and research support services, including reference consultation, literature search, information literacy training, professional database training, reference book services, and more. In addition, the center offers a variety of study spaces, including independent study areas, group study areas, computer rooms, and research rooms, among others, to meet the needs of people with different learning needs. In addition to traditional library services, Djanogly LRC also regularly organizes various academic and cultural events such as lectures, workshops, exhibitions and performances to provide rich learning and cultural exchange opportunities for students and staff.
